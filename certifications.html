CompTIA Security+ SY0-601 Certification Prep.

JANUARY 2024 - MARCH 2024

I gave myself three months to complete the prep work for the Security+ exam. Had I known better at the time I may have done the prep work at the same time as I was doing the Google Security course work as it did cover most of the same material. There was no formal training that I used for this phase of study. I bought an industry leading textbook (physical copy), as well as some exam prep material (Professor Messer), watched a bunch of YouTube videos and made lots of notes. My personal goal was to achieve 85% on the exam. Listed below are some one the actual steps and material I used. I decided to take the SY0-601 instead of the newly created SY0-701 because of the amount of material available for the older cert. SY0-601 will be retired in July 2024 but passing this exam still grants the student the full Security+ designation.

Exam Prep Process

  • CompTIA Security+ SY0-601 Exam Objectives - from the official site.
    • This is a PDF that covers every subject that can appear on the exam. If you take this document alone and define and memorize every item on the list, you will pass the exam. You are required to provide your email or login to obtain the objectives, but you will need to have a relationship with CompTIA if you plan to write the exam anyway.
  • Book - CompTIA Security+ All-in-One Exam Guide, Sixth Edition ISBN-13 978-1260464009
    • I read this book from start to finish three times. It is laid out in the same order as the exam objective published by CompTIA. The chapters and domains are well designed, and I found it easy to read even while being extremely dry subject matter. The practice question contained in the book are awkwardly worded at times.
  • Professor Messer SY0-601 Practice Exams - Availible from the Professor Messer website.
    • These practice exams are written in a style that is very close to the questions on the actual exam. I used a spread sheet to keep track of the answers and the question domain so that I could easily track which area I need to review. This material contains detailed explanations of each correct and incorrect answer and I found this extremely useful for review purposes.
  • Professor Messer's CompTIA Security+ Training Course
    • This is a playlist of 177 free videos (YouTube) that go over every item in the official exam objectives in depth. I watched them after reading each domain in the book. I found it helpful to take notes as I watched. They are arranged in short 5-10 minute segments so I was able to rewatch and review subjects I was struggling with.

Google Cybersecurity Certification - Coursera

NOVEMBER 2023 - DECEMBER 2023

The Google Cybersecurity Certificate has eight courses that focus and build upon core concepts and skills related to the daily work of cybersecurity professionals, including foundational cybersecurity models and frameworks that are used to mitigate risk; protecting networks and data; using programming to automate tasks; identifying and responding to security incidents; and communicating and collaborating with stakeholders. Additionally, you will apply what you’ve learned in each course by completing portfolio projects that can be used to showcase your understanding of essential cybersecurity concepts to potential employers.

Required Courses

  1. Foundations of Cybersecurity
  2. Play It Safe: Manage Security Risks
  3. Connect and Protect: Networks and Network Security
  4. Tools of the Trade: Linux and SQL
  5. Assests, Threats, and Vulnerabilities
  6. Sound the Alarm: Detection and Response
  7. Automate Cybersecurity Tasks with Python
  8. Put it to Work: Prepare for Cybersecurity Jobs
