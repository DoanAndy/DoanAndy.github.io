Fleet Tracker

I built an interactive fleet tracking in Google sheets. Milage data is fed by employees via a web form and it updates the dashboard to track service intervals. The sheet also links to important fleet information that is used to keep track of maintenance costs.

  • Status: Complete
  • Category: Business Process
  • Domain: Spread Sheet
  • Toolset: XLOOKUP, Web Forms, Google Sheets

Background

We deploy a fleet of eleven commercial vehicles on a daily basis. We needed an easy way to track service intervals and maintenance costs for all vehicles that was easy to update and share across the management team and ownership. There was also a need to have an easy place to cross reference plate numbers, unit number and VINs for operational needs. Have all relevant data on hand makes it much easier to order parts and work with service vendors.

In order for this tool to be useful it needed to be easily accessed on various devices particularly mobile devices. Although this data could be considered sensitive there is no exposure of any PII or data linking vehicle VIN numbers back to a particular business entity. The decsion was made to deploy this tool on Google Sheets for is availiblity, compatibility, cost and relative security.
