ZAXL Del Inc.

APRIL 2021 - CURRENT

In late 2020 I was put in contact with Sunjay Nath via LinkedIn. He was in the process of purchasing an existing FedEx Ground contract and he needed someone to run it for him. Although at the time I was happy with my position at FedEx a lot of what Sunjay presented as his business plan aligned perfectly with my personal values. With my knowledge of FedEx contracts and with the management structure of the new company I felt correctly so that I would thrive in this endeavor. After getting through the start-up phase of the business through the first year the plan was to look aggressively at grow and expand into other geographic areas. Unfortunately, in the summer of 2023 as part of a reorganization plan FedEx made an announcement, they would discontinue all FedEx Ground contracts in Canada as of 2024 and switch to an employee driver model. Although this is not the way I was hoping for this to end I am still happy with my decision, and I am grateful for the opportunities given to me by Sunjay (ZAXL) and FedEx Ground.
